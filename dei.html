My Commitment to D.E.I.

I would like to start by ackowledging that my journey thus far in Astronomy and academia has been from a position of privilege. I have spent my career learning about the consequences of systemic racism and rampant discrimiation without ever having to experience it first hand. I need to do my part to eliminate discriminatory practices and bring positive changes to those affected by these systems. Below is a non-exhaustive list of actions I have and will continue to take throughout my career.

  • Continuely educate myself so that I may be a better ally for marginalized groups
  • Speak up and speak out against discrimiation I see and hear about
  • Hold myself and others accountable for what we say and do
  • Actively listen to those living with the repercussions of our broken system
  • Ensure that I contribute to an inclusive and safe learning environment
